Welcome to the Admin Console, your centralized interface for managing the tools, data, and settings that keep your operation running smoothly. This console provides quick access to essential controls such as reports, user permissions, and system preferences.

Whether you're monitoring performance metrics, configuring settings, or reviewing user activity, the Admin Console gives you the flexibility and structure to stay in control. The left-hand sidebar provides a consistent navigation anchor that gives you quick access to the tools you need.
